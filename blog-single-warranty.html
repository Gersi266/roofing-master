The Dynasty Roofing Difference: Our 10-Year Labor Warranty

When investing in a new roof or significant roofing repairs, the quality of the installation is just as important as the quality of the materials used. At Dynasty Roofing & Contracting Inc., we stand behind our workmanship with confidence, which is why we offer a comprehensive 10-year labor warranty on all our roofing installations.

Understanding Roofing Warranties

Before discussing what makes our warranty special, it's important to understand the two main types of roofing warranties:

Manufacturer's Warranty: This covers defects in the roofing materials themselves. Most quality shingles come with warranties ranging from 25-50 years, depending on the product. However, these warranties typically only cover the materials, not the labor to replace them.

Labor/Workmanship Warranty: This covers issues that arise from improper installation. If your roof develops problems due to installation errors, this warranty covers the cost of repairs, including labor and materials.

Many roofing companies offer limited labor warranties of just 1-5 years. At Dynasty Roofing, we go above and beyond with our 10-year labor warranty, demonstrating our confidence in the quality of our work.

What Our 10-Year Labor Warranty Covers

Our comprehensive labor warranty covers issues related to the installation of your roof, including:

  • Leaks resulting from improper installation
  • Improper flashing installation
  • Incorrect nailing patterns or techniques
  • Improper sealing
  • Inadequate ventilation installation
  • Other workmanship-related issues that affect the performance of your roof

If any of these issues arise within 10 years of installation, we will repair or replace the affected areas at no cost to you.

Why a Strong Labor Warranty Matters

A roof is only as good as its installation. Even the highest quality materials will fail prematurely if they're not installed correctly. Here's why our 10-year labor warranty provides significant value:

  • Peace of mind: You can rest easy knowing that your investment is protected for a decade
  • Financial protection: Roof repairs can be costly, but our warranty shields you from unexpected expenses due to installation errors
  • Transferability: Our warranty can be transferred to a new homeowner if you sell your property, adding value to your home
  • Accountability: Our warranty demonstrates our commitment to quality workmanship and customer satisfaction

To ensure you get the most from your roofing warranty, we recommend:

  • Keeping your roof clean and free of debris
