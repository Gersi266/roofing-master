Cedar Roofing: Natural Beauty and Durability

Cedar roofing has been a premium choice for discerning homeowners for centuries, and for good reason. At Dynasty Roofing & Contracting Inc., we specialize in cedar roof installation and maintenance, helping homeowners across the Durham Region and GTA enhance their homes with this timeless roofing material.

The Timeless Appeal of Cedar

Cedar roofing offers a natural beauty that simply can't be replicated by synthetic materials. Each cedar shake or shingle has its own unique grain pattern and color variations, creating a roof with character and depth. As cedar ages, it weathers to a distinguished silver-gray patina that adds to its charm and elegance.

Beyond aesthetics, cedar roofing offers several practical advantages:

Natural Durability

Cedar contains natural oils and resins that make it naturally resistant to:

  • Moisture damage and rot
  • Insect infestations, including termites
  • UV radiation damage
  • Bacterial growth and decay

With proper installation and maintenance, a cedar roof can last 30-50 years, making it a long-term investment in your home's beauty and protection.

Energy Efficiency

Cedar is a natural insulator, providing up to twice the insulation value of asphalt shingles. This natural insulating property helps keep your home cooler in summer and warmer in winter, potentially reducing your energy costs throughout the year.

Environmental Benefits

For environmentally conscious homeowners, cedar offers significant advantages:

  • It's a renewable resource when harvested from responsibly managed forests
  • Cedar roofing has a smaller carbon footprint compared to synthetic materials
  • At the end of its life cycle, cedar is biodegradable
  • The production process requires less energy than manufacturing synthetic alternatives

Types of Cedar Roofing

At Dynasty Roofing, we offer two main types of cedar roofing:

Cedar Shakes

Cedar shakes are hand-split, giving them a rustic, textured appearance with varying thicknesses. They create a more rugged, three-dimensional look that adds character and shadow lines to your roof. Shakes are thicker than shingles and offer excellent weather resistance.

Cedar Shingles

Cedar shingles are machine-cut for a more uniform, smooth appearance. They're thinner than shakes and lie flatter against the roof deck, creating a sleeker, more refined look. Shingles are ideal for homes with a more formal architectural style.

Maintenance Considerations

While cedar roofing is naturally durable, proper maintenance will maximize its lifespan:

  • Regular cleaning to remove debris, moss, and algae
  • Ensuring proper ventilation in the attic to prevent moisture buildup
  • Periodic treatments with preservatives to enhance the cedar's natural resistance
  • Prompt repairs of any damaged shakes or shingles
