Skylight Installation: Bringing Natural Light Into Your Home

Skylights are an excellent way to enhance natural lighting in your home or business. At Dynasty Roofing & Contracting Inc., we specialize in professional skylight installations that not only brighten your living space but also improve energy efficiency and indoor comfort.

The Benefits of Skylight Installation

Adding skylights to your home offers numerous advantages that go beyond simply letting in more light:

  • Enhanced natural lighting: Skylights can provide up to 30% more light than vertical windows of the same size, brightening dark hallways, bathrooms, and interior rooms
  • Energy savings: Properly positioned skylights can reduce the need for artificial lighting during daylight hours, lowering electricity costs
  • Improved ventilation: Operable skylights can be opened to release hot air that rises to the ceiling, improving air circulation and cooling your home naturally
  • Increased vitamin D exposure: Natural sunlight helps your body produce vitamin D, which is essential for bone health and immune function
  • Enhanced mood and productivity: Studies show that exposure to natural light improves mood, reduces stress, and increases productivity
  • Aesthetic appeal: Skylights add architectural interest and can make rooms feel larger and more open

Types of Skylights

When considering skylight installation, you have several options to choose from:

Fixed Skylights:

  • Do not open or close
  • Provide consistent natural light
  • More energy-efficient and less prone to leaks than operable models
  • Ideal for areas where ventilation isn't a priority

Ventilating Skylights:

  • Can be opened to allow fresh air and release heat
  • Available with manual or electric operation
  • Some models include rain sensors that automatically close the skylight when it rains
  • Perfect for kitchens, bathrooms, and other areas that benefit from additional ventilation

Tubular Skylights:

  • Smaller diameter tubes that channel light from the roof to the ceiling
  • Ideal for smaller spaces or areas where a traditional skylight isn't practical
  • Less expensive and easier to install than conventional skylights
  • Minimal heat gain or loss compared to larger skylights

Energy Efficiency Considerations

Modern skylights are designed with energy efficiency in mind:

  • Energy Star rated: Look for Energy Star certified skylights that meet strict energy efficiency guidelines
  • Low-E glass: Low-emissivity coatings reduce heat transfer while allowing light to pass through
  • Double or triple glazing: Multiple panes with insulating gas between them improve thermal performance
  • Proper placement: South-facing skylights maximize winter heat gain, while north-facing skylights provide consistent, glare-free light
  • Shading options: Blinds or shades can be added to control light and heat gain during summer months

Professional Installation is Key

While skylights offer numerous benefits, proper installation is crucial to prevent leaks and ensure optimal performance. At Dynasty Roofing & Contracting Inc., our professional installations include:

  • Expert assessment to determine the best location and type of skylight for your needs
  • Proper flashing and sealing to prevent water infiltration
  • Insulation around the skylight shaft to minimize heat loss
  • Interior finishing that complements your home's aesthetic
  • Our 10-year labor warranty for peace of mind

With our experienced team handling your skylight installation, you can enjoy all the benefits of natural light without worrying about leaks or other common issues associated with improper installation.

Is Skylight Installation Right for Your Home?

Skylights can enhance almost any home, but they're particularly beneficial if:

  • Your home has dark areas that would benefit from additional natural light
  • You want to reduce your reliance on artificial lighting
  • You're looking to improve ventilation in bathrooms or kitchens
  • You want to add architectural interest to your home
  • You're interested in the psychological benefits of increased natural light
